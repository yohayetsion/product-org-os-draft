The book this is built on

Product Org OS v5.0 is the runnable substrate of Vision to Value: Your Operating System for Running a World-Class Product Organization, Volume One in the Executive Operating System Series. The book frames the operating model. The OS implements it as agents, skills, and the runnable substrate that makes Charters in the Decision Provenance Standard achievable at Conformance Level 3.

Section 6 of the Standard grades the Charter; the Standard does not certify. Conformance is a deployer-side achievement, not an OS feature. Charters authored using the OS can achieve Level 3 when deployer-side discipline matches the OS's structural support.
